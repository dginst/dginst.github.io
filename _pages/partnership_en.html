Partnership

As the labour and capital markets (industrial, financial and venture capital industries) show strong and increasing demand for qualified technicians in the field of bitcoin and blockchain, Digital Gold Institute is creating a centre of excellence which is recognised internationally as the incubator hub for technicians and innovators.

The institute’s core activities focus on research, development, training and corporate relations with attention to the partners of the institute and in synergy with the part of the academic world which is showing attention and interest to the phenomenon of bitcoin e blockchain (for example, Milano-Bicocca, Politecnico di Milano, ESSEC, etc.).

The collaboration with researchers and students in thesis projects and internship gives the institute the privilege of preparing a dozen qualified professionals per year, generating a network of alumni which creates an ecosystem of proficiency and innovation. The experience at the Digital Gold Institute is called a “spin cycle”: the place where one step by in order to reach other shores, perhaps international.

The institute is sustained by partnershipwith strategic corporations and by client collaborations (list available upon request). To our partners we offer value benefits:

  • A full day each quarter, to be used for any of the following purposes: consulting, project management supervision, institutional relations, training, etc.
  • Free access to all the training activities hosted by the institute, for 2 people
  • Exclusive access to the DGI report about bitcoin and blockchain: ecosystem, market, technology and regulation
  • Publication of the partner’s logo on the website of the institute and in selective public initiatives, brochures, etc.
  • The opportunity to orientate the institute research activities in favour of the partner’s priorities and interests, although the independence of the Institute's scientific directorate is preserved.
  • Privileged dialogue with the institute's researchers and students in function of eventual fellowship, internship, recruitment, etc.

Prospective partners can contact the institute to discuss the opportunities of partnership.
