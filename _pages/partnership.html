Partnership Program

The Institute has a strategic long-term corporate partnership program.

Our core activities focus on consulting, training, research, and development, in cooperation with the academic world that pays attention to bitcoin and blockchain (e.g. Milano-Bicocca, Politecnico di Milano, ESSEC, etc.). The quality of our initiatives enables us to provide values to our partners; namely, we offer to them:

  • A full day each quarter, to be used for consulting, project management, institutional relations, training, etc.
  • Free access for 2 people to all the training activities organized by the Institute
  • Exclusive access to the DGI Quarterly Report about bitcoin and blockchain: ecosystem, market, technology and regulation
  • Partner’s logo visibility on our website and in selected public initiatives, brochures, etc.
  • The opportunity to influence our research according to partner’s priorities and interests (respecting, of course, the independence of the Institute's scientific mission)

As the labour and capital markets show strong and increasing demand for qualified professionals in the field of bitcoin and blockchain, Digital Gold Institute is a centre of excellence recognised internationally as incubator hub for technicians and innovators. Our partners enjoy a privileged dialogue with our researchers and students, benefitting from early recruitment opportunities.

Prospective partners can contact the Institute to discuss opportunities for partnership.
