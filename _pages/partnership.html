Partnership Program

Becoming one of our partners means empowering your business with a proper understanding of Bitcoin, crypto assets, and blockchain technology. It is a strategic choice that will allow you to leverage unique opportunities while avoiding the irrational hype that pollutes any attempt to master these topics.

Our partnership program is aimed at consulting firms, financial institutions, insurance companies, innovative enterprises, corporates, lobbying groups, and regulators.

We offer our partners a continuous support in term of consulting, training, research, and development, in cooperation with the academic world that pays attention to Bitcoin and blockchain. We help our partners in starting-up their first project, guiding and training them to reach full autonomy.

Our basic partnership agreement includes:

  • A full day each quarter, to be used for consulting, project management, institutional relations, training, etc.
  • Free access for 2 people to all the initiatives organized by the Institute
  • Exclusive access to our Quarterly Report about Bitcoin and blockchain: ecosystem, market, technology, and regulation
  • Partner’s logo visibility on our website and in selected public initiatives, brochures, etc.
  • The opportunity to influence our research according to partner’s priorities and interests (respecting, of course, the independence of the Institute's scientific mission)
  • A privileged dialogue with our researchers and students, benefitting from early recruitment opportunities

Prospective partners can contact the Institute to discuss the available opportunities.
