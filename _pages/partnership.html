Partnership Program

DGI has a partnership program aimed at consulting firms, lobbying groups, innovative enterprises, corporates, financial institutions, insurance companies, and regulators.

We provide consulting, training, research, and development, in cooperation with the academic world that pays attention to Bitcoin and blockchain; namely, we offer:

  • A full day each quarter, to be used for consulting, project management, institutional relations, training, etc.
  • Free access for 2 people to all the training activities organized by the Institute
  • Exclusive access to the DGI Quarterly Report about Bitcoin and blockchain: ecosystem, market, technology, and regulation
  • Partner’s logo visibility on our website and in selected public initiatives, brochures, etc.
  • The opportunity to influence our research according to partner’s priorities and interests (respecting, of course, the independence of the Institute's scientific mission)
  • A privileged dialogue with our researchers and students, benefitting from early recruitment opportunities

Prospective partners can contact the Institute to discuss the available opportunities.
